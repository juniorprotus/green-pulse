GreenPulse - Smart Waste Management System


About
GreenPulse is a smart waste management system designed to
help citizens and municipalities manage waste collection 
efficiently. Citizens can report waste issues,
view collection schedules, and receive waste management tips.
Administrators can manage reports,
create collection schedules, and view statistics.
